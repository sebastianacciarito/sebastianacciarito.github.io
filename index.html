The statistics of current performance reviews processes

53% of employers said they don't actively track improved performance
45% of HR leaders do not think annual performance reviews are an accurate appraisal for employee’s work
Only 8% of companies believe their performance management process is highly effective in driving business value, while 58% say it’s not an effective use of time.
58% of organizations continue to use spreadsheets as their primary way to track performance metrics

How Workthrive solves these problems

To solve these problems, we have implemented the following features to make your life easier

Goal Based Metrics

Understand what your employees are striving towards at a simple glance. Our metrics enable you to track,compare and analyse how your employees are complete their goals.

Ondemand Performance Reviews

Preparing performance review documents are not a good use of your time. Thats why we have created an api that allows you to export an employees achievements at the click of a button.

Track the goals of entire teams

It's not only important to track the goals of employees but also track how your whole team is tracking toward your goal. Thats why we have engineered our system to track not only employee but also team's goals and performance.

It's on the cloud

No need for expensive servers in your office, we host everything on the cloud securely, so that you don't have to.
